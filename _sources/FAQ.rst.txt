Our `reddit community <https://www.reddit.com/r/stroscot>`__ (checked

How do I contribute?
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Any and all comments will be taken into consideration. Commit access is
available for anyone with 2 or more commits to the project.
